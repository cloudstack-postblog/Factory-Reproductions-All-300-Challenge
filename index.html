Factory Reproductions: All 300 Challenge

September 2, 2024

Final results of the 2021 “All 300 Challenge”! Take a look at this Procharged 2006 300 SRT, with our FR77 Hellcat HC2 Replica Wheels. Check out the full recap!

2006 Chrysler 300 SRT

The winner of the 2021 Factory Reproductions “All 300 Challenge” is Ryan S, known on Instagram as @shenanigans_89. Check out some great shots of his 2006 300 SRT, Procharged, with a High Horsepower Performance “Rumbler” cam and Getrag swapped rear differential. Some of his sponsors include Lightingtrendz, Flow series halos, and 4W Demon eyes. He’s running a staggered set of our FR77 Hellcat HC2 reps wrapped in Nitto NT555 G2s.
